### About

DripX is a token-streaming platform that facilitates the gradual unlocking of tokens over time. Token owners can setup Streams assigned to Algorand account recipients who are entitled to withdraw funds at a throttled rate over a time period defined by the original token owner

### Involvement

Creator, smart contract/frontend developer, and UI designer.

**Frontend**: React, TypeScript, Next.js, Jest, ChakraIU/Emotion, Redux Toolkit
